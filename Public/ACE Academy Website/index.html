Why Choose the Academy of Cleaning Excellence?

Managing risk and protecting human health within built environments, using progressive knowledge and proactive processes, is job ONE.

Certifications establish professionalism and advance an individual's value in the workplace. Annually, over 5,000 students from around the globe complete Academy of Cleaning Excellence (A.C.E.) certification courses.

The Academy provides professional development education to facility maintenance operations around the globe through live, online and Virtual instruction. Of the 15 Academy of Cleaning certification courses, 3 are ISSA, CITS verified; Green Technician, Master Technician and Environmental Services Technician.

ISSA Master Technician Certification

During this certification Course you will

  • • earn 9 ISSA certificates, one being an ISSA Master,
  • • outline a roadmap for your progress,
  • • detail how to itemize your work schedule,
  • • transform your thinking about Green cleaning,
  • • and you will create a draft of your new "WOW" statement presentation and sell sheet.
