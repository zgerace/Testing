Cleaning services are in a daily battle against an unseen enemy, pathogenic bacteria.

These illness causing microorganisms are all around us and preventing them from infecting the public is the reason why you are a key part of the Risk Management team of ANY facility.

Knowing the ‘whys’ of environmental health services, advances the abilities of not only the individual, but the operation providing these specialized services.

EVERY facility, no matter the type of facility, requires a focus to protect the health of the individuals and the business as a whole.

This Environmental Hygienist certification course is designed to inform the student on the particulars required to provide services for daycare, schools, doctors clinics, and many other locations.
